Predictive Maintenance of Heavy Industry Machinery

The objective is to predict whether a machine will have a power failure, PWF, in the production process. This failure mode can be described as, given the product quality (Low, Medium, High), the torque [Nm] and the rotational speed [rpm], if the power required is below 3500 W or above 9000 W, the machine will face a power failure.

Prediction

The entry for the product quality will be as followed: Low = 0; Medium = 1; High = 2.

The output power is in natural log form. The power failure limits of 3500 W - 9000 W turn into 8.16 W - 9.10 W.
